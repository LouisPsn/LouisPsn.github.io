My Portfolio Some Of My Works

Shape Generator

The Geographic Layer Merging Tool project, developed during my internship at Setics, involved creating an innovative tool used in Setics Fiberstate for monitoring fiber optic projects. This tool was designed to merge geographic layers efficiently, enhancing the overall effectiveness of the monitoring process. By fine-tuning the Python code, the tool's efficiency and user satisfaction were significantly boosted.

Performance Logs Parser

The Performance Logs Parser tool project was developed during my internship at Setics in the summer of 2023. The tool was designed to analyze the performance of Setics Sttar, an optical fiber design tool. It involved writing Python software to parse and analyze large datasets, providing insights into the software's performance. The project was part of a collaborative effort within an international team, adhering to agile methodologies to enhance team efficiency and software quality.

French Robotics Cup 2nd iteration : The Cherry on the cake

In my second year at ENSEIRB-MATMECA, I continued my involvement in the French Robotics Cup with the Eirbot association. From April 2022 to June 2023, I was part of a team that developed Arduino LED display modules for a competition robot. This project involved designing and implementing displays that conveyed crucial information from the robot, integrating technical expertise in software programming and hardware design with collaboration and problem-solving skills. It was a demanding yet fulfilling experience that enabled me to enhance my abilities in robotics and teamwork-oriented engineering.

Yearbook

The Yearbook Design project was undertaken as part of my role as Yearbook Manager for a GALA association from August 2022 to February 2023. The objective was to create a yearbook that would serve as a keepsake for future graduates. This project involved coordinating the content curation, design, and production processes to ensure the creation of a high-quality publication. The yearbook aimed to capture the memories and achievements of the graduating class, providing a tangible reminder of their time at the institution.

Eirbot Website

The Eirbot Website project was undertaken from July 2022 to August 2023, where I was responsible for designing and launching the new website for the Eirbot association using WordPress. This project involved creating a user-friendly, visually appealing, and functional website that aligned with the organizational needs and goals of the association.

VBA Macro

During my internship at Praxair MRC in June and July 2022, I developed a VBA macro in Microsoft Excel aimed at streamlining production processes. This innovative tool played a crucial role in enhancing production efficiency and elevating overall quality standards. The macro automated various tasks, reducing manual effort and minimizing errors, thereby contributing to a more efficient and reliable production workflow.

French Robotics Cup 1st iteration : Age of Bots

In my first year at ENSEIRB-MATMECA, I had the opportunity to participate in the French Robotics Cup with the Eirbot association. From April 2022 to June 2023, I played a role in the team and developed an Arduino LED display module for the competition. This project involved creating displays that provided essential information from the robot, combining technical skills in software programming and hardware design with teamwork and problem-solving abilities. It was an enriching experience that allowed me to apply my theoretical knowledge in a practical, competitive setting and further develop my skills in robotics and team collaboration.

Conception and Creation of an "Otto" Robot

The "Otto" robot project was a notable endeavor undertaken by me as part of my involvement with Eirbot, a robotics association. Over the course of February to March 2022, I successfully designed and built an "Otto" robot that was capable of executing precise actions and detecting obstacles using an ultrasonic sensor. This project not only honed my skills in robotics and sensor integration but also enhanced my ability to develop solutions that interact effectively with their environment. It was a valuable experience that contributed significantly to my proficiency in robotics engineering.
